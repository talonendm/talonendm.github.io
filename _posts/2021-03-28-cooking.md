#### Minty Greek Dipping Sauce (FI / EN)

Ingredients:

- 2 dl Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp mint jelly
- 1 clove of garlic, crushed
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Let the sauce sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
